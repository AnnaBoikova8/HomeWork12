About me

Hello! My name is Anya, but in another world - the motoworld my name is Sam. At the age of 29, learned to fall and get up, both in life and on a motorcycle. We, brutal bikers, but do not pass by a cat, dog, etc. And be sure to stroke and feed! There is a law in life: when you get some kind of profit, you must definitely share it with the needy! Oh yes, my family is a husband, a cat and, of course, 2 iron horses!

Dating history

Life with the roaring sound of the engine began in 2016. My prince came on a horse, albeit an iron K-750. We managed to ride it for over a year without feeling our legs and with ringing in our ears.

After there was the next purchase of an iron horse - Drag Star. The path began in 2017 from the city of Bakhmut to Cherkasy. 600 km with many roadblocks, police, loss of a mirror, beautiful scenery and body pain. If you take hardcore - a trip Cherkasy-Kherson-Odessa-Cherkasy in two days.

The beginning of 2020 was a new addition to our Virago family - this is my, my Witch. The young lady forgives absolutely everything, even driving Odessa-Cherkassy on a burned-out generator.

In 2021, there was a big replenishment of Harley Davidson and, unfortunately, has been suspended for now.

Riding is wonderful, it's freedom, beautiful scenery, rock in the ears, euphoria in the chest. Motorcycle - a hobby that has grown into a lifestyle!
